# Data Retention Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-19
Owner: platform-owner

## Scope
Applies to lead and message data collected through the production website.

## Retention Windows
- lead submissions: `180 days`
- contact/messages records: `90 days`

## Handling After Expiry
- records are deleted or anonymized after retention expiry.
- any temporary exports/log extracts must follow the same maximum retention boundaries.

## Access & Security
- access is restricted to authorized operational owners.
- sensitive operational evidence remains in secure owner-controlled storage.

## Change Control
Any change to this policy must be documented in `docs/runtime/OWNER_INPUT_QUEUE.md` and reflected in runtime/governance evidence.
