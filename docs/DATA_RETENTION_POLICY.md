# Data Retention Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-19

## Lead Data
Lead submissions are retained for 180 days from submission date.
If no engagement is established within this period, data is automatically deleted or anonymized.

## General Messages
Non-qualified public messages are retained for 90 days.
After expiration, data is automatically deleted.

## Active Client Data
Client data is retained for the duration of the contract and 12 months after project completion for documentation and legal purposes.

## Data Handling
- Data is stored securely.
- Access is restricted to the owner.
- No data is shared with third parties.
- Automated deletion or anonymization is applied after expiration.
